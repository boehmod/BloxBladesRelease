What is Blox'n'Blades?

Blox'n'Blades is a unique and original Minecraft PvP matchmaking, consisting of several game-modes & dozens of maps to bring a brand new experience within the game. Blox'n'Blades also gives players the ability to manage a friends list, play in a group/party and join in with the community for events and new features everyday!
